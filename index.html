Success calculator dashboard

If user have account without Telemetry than data filled in part “Filters” of a dashboard will be used in calculations of most parts of Dashboard.
If user have account with Telemetry than data filled in part “Filters” of a dashboard will be used in calculations of planned revenue – part Break-even point of a dashboard.

Dashboard consists of 6 parts: Filters; Information; Sell in\out - P&L; Break-even point; ROI; Other Benefits.
